THINK GLOBAL ACT LOCAL

As the name suggests, the Badaun Institute of Management & Technology, utilizes global knowledge for local solutions. BIMT prepares students to make the most out of an increasingly connected world. This is achieved by BIMT’s effective network, our curriculum emphasizing global knowledge, and our background in local and cross-cultural communication.

MULTIPLE MODES OF LEARNING

Every human is different. While BIMT utilizes time-tested pedagogical methods, We allow students to learn in the way that is most effective for them through innovative learning techniques. This is why BIMT applies the multiples intelligences approach.

HARD & SOFT SKILLS COMBINED

BIMT Provides students with the information and pragmatic skills necessary to access opportunities. But we place equal importance on training students to think critically, work creatively and act justly. These educational qualities will help students succeed in their future employment BIMT’s highest hope is that our curriculum will help students to think boldly and wisely in order to make a difference in their local community.
