DIRECTOR’S MESSAGE

Success grows with the drive & energy of students and leadership of mentors. These factors when put in the right direction make the vision clear. As the vision grows , the mentors push and encourage their students. They provide the plan of attack. We at BIMT strongly believe in student and mentor interaction to motivate students to channelize their energy to achieve outstanding results.

Bachelor’s degree is the first step in the professional education of a student so we lay emphasis on matching the abilities of students with their interests to counsel for career options in the future. We make endeavor to start workshop by grouping students based on their career goals. Visit of other university professors to develope research skills for students who wishes to pursue higher education. We emphasize management and professional skills in students by organize group discussion. BIMT offers a lot and expect you to be motivated.

It is for you to decide what will be your next step to stand on your feet and we will show you the path to follow. Welcome to all.
